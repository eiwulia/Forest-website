About Us

Wood is a renewable, competitive and environment-friendly material, it is a basic need of modern society, and it contributes to quality of life. Forest.Inc manages a range of forests each of which is managed in a sustainable, efficient and socially responsible manner.

A large proportion of our forest land has been created through afforestation projects, contributing to an increase in the world's forest area, and locking up and storing carbon dioxide in trees. Forest.Inc ambition is to be a leader in environment responsibility and to be recognised for this.

Our Values

We manage our forests with openness, effectiveness and accountability and strive to be the best in class regarding sustainable forest management. We do our utmost to choose and implement modern, efficient and environmentally effective management regimes and technologies. We work with research and development to minimise any negative effect that our operations could potentially have on biodiversity and environs. We evaluate environmental performance when selecting suppliers, contractors and business partners.
